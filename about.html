About Urban Threads

Urban Threads is a project clothing store I created as a class project for my IT 240 Web Fluency. This site demonstrates clean design, category organization, and real-world product page structure.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide users with a simple and modern online shopping experience. Urban Threads uses minimal design, realistic product layouts, and clean navigation.
